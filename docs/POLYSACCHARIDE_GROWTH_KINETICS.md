$$V_{growth} = V_{max\_human} \cdot \left( 1 + \alpha \cdot \frac{[P_{bamboo}]}{K_p + [P_{bamboo}]} \right)$$

*   $\alpha$ = **The Acceleration Multiplier (Target: 2.45 – 3.10).** This represents the speed multiplier where plant-derived components push the cell division cycle faster than normal human baseline limits.
*   $K_p$ = Affinity constant of the polysaccharide to the target binding site.
